about me

Let Me Introduce Myself

Introducing MACZITO, the sonic visionary hailing from Spruitview, driven by a relentless mission to craft timeless beats. With two stellar EPs, including the widely acclaimed "SOUL SEARCHING" released with the prestigious Sculptured Records, MACZITO's remixes, such as the one for Edsoul ft Moresoul, have become fan favorites.

Prepare for the upcoming sonic journey as MACZITO unveils "PRAY" on a distinguished British compilation. Looking ahead into 2023, anticipate a second EP that solidifies MACZITO's commitment to pushing the boundaries of deep and soulful house music. In MACZITO's world, the beats are bold, the goals are grand, and the funk is forever.

View My Discography
2011
Setting Sail on My Musical Odyssey

In 2011, I embarked on my musical journey, exploring the art of mixing with CDJs. Over time, this passion led to the creation of the DJ group Soul Selectives with a friend. Although my friend later shifted focus, I continued the journey, reshaping Soul Selectives with a new partner, forging ahead in the dynamic world of DJing.

2013
Embracing the Academy of Sound Engineering

Following the completion of my matriculation, I enrolled in the Academy of Sound Engineering (ASE) to delve into the intricacies of sound engineering.

2015
Breaking into the Scene

n 2015, I landed my inaugural gig alongside a close friend, who happened to be the younger brother of Sculptured Music. Together, we formed the dynamic duo "Soul Electives" to kickstart our musical journey.

2021
DEP Debut - "Away"

In 2021, I made a splash in the music industry with the release of my debut EP, "Away." Featuring three standout tracks, this mixtape propelled me into the limelight, marking a significant milestone in my musical journey.

2022
Collaborating with Soul Candy

In 2022, I joined forces with Soul Candy for a groundbreaking collaboration, culminating in the release of our track, "Different You." This partnership marked an exciting chapter in my musical exploration.

2023
EP Sensation - "Soul Searching"

In 2023, I teamed up with Sculptured Music to unleash the EP "Soul Searching," featuring four explosive tracks. The release took the music scene by storm, garnering widespread acclaim and a phenomenal reception.
